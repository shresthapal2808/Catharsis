About Us

Who We Are

CATHARSIS is a mental wellness platform built to offer support, guidance, and emotional care. We aim to foster a safe space where anyone can seek help, be heard, and take steps toward healing.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, stigma-free mental health support through compassionate listeners and certified therapists. We believe everyone deserves a chance to be heard, without fear or shame.

Our Vision

We envision a world where emotional expression is normalized and help is always within reach. Catharsis connects people to the right support, encouraging openness, empathy, and well-being.
